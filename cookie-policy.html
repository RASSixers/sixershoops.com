What are cookies? Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

1. How We Use Cookies

SixersHoops.com uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you interact with our site
  • Improve site performance and functionality
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

5. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please note that:

  • Some features of the website may not function properly
  • Your preferences and settings will not be saved
  • You may see less relevant advertisements
  • We cannot remember your login status (if applicable)
  • Site performance may be affected

Note: Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to function. Disabling them may prevent you from using certain features.

6. Other Tracking Technologies

6.1 Web Beacons (Pixels)

We may use web beacons (also called pixels or clear GIFs) to track user behavior and measure the effectiveness of our content and advertising.

6.2 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save preferences and improve performance. This data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared through your browser settings.

6.3 Session Storage

Temporary data stored during your browsing session. This is automatically deleted when you close your browser.

7. Cookie Duration

Cookies are classified by duration:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted
