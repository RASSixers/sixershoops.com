Sixers vs Bulls Player Grades:

Sixers Fall to Bulls 113-111

November 4, 2025

Philadelphia 76ers Player Grades

Starters

Tyrese Maxey

A+
39 PTS • 3 REB • 4 AST • 2 STL • 14-26 FG • 6-10 3PT • 5-6 FT

Maxey was absolutely incredible, scoring 39 points and keeping the Sixers ahead for most of the game. He put the team on his back in the first quarter with 12 points and continued attacking all night.

The fact that they blew a 24-point lead isn't on him. One of his best performances of the season.

VJ Edgecombe

B+
12 PTS • 11 REB • 2 AST • 2 STL • 5-15 FG • 1-5 3PT • 1-2 FT

VJ grabbed his first double-double with 12 points and 11 boards. The shooting wasn't great at 33%, but he competed hard on the glass and made plays defensively with 2 steals.

Kelly Oubre Jr.

B
18 PTS • 5 REB • 2 AST • 1 STL • 3 BLK • 6-14 FG • 2-5 3PT • 4-4 FT

Oubre had 18 points and 3 blocks, playing solid two-way basketball. He helped build that big lead early but couldn't stop the collapse in the fourth.

Joel Embiid

C+
20 PTS • 9 REB • 2 AST • 2 STL • 5 BLK • 8-19 FG • 1-9 3PT • 3-4 FT

Embiid had 20 points and 5 blocks, but he went 1-for-9 from three and struggled down the stretch. The defense wasn't there when it mattered most in the fourth quarter.

Jabari Walker

C-
2 PTS • 5 REB • 1 STL • 2 BLK • 1-5 FG • 0-1 3PT

Walker only scored 2 points on 1-for-5 shooting. He grabbed 5 boards and had 2 blocks, but the offensive production just isn't there.

Bench

C-
13 PTS • 5 REB • 3 AST • 3 STL • 5-11 FG • 2-6 3PT • 1-2 FT

Trendon Watford

C+
