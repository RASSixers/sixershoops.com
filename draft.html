Draft Pick Notes & Protections

2026:

  • If the 76ers’ pick lands between 1 and 4, Philadelphia keeps the pick.
  • If the pick lands 5 through 30, it goes to the Oklahoma City Thunder.
  • If the pick is not conveyed in 2026, the same protection applies in 2027.
  • If it still doesn’t convey in 2027, it converts into a 2027 2nd-round pick for OKC.

2027:

  • This pick only conveys if the 2026 pick to OKC has already been sent.
  • If the 2027 pick lands between 1 and 8, Philadelphia keeps it. If it lands 9 through 30, it goes to the Brooklyn Nets.
  • If not conveyed in 2027, same protection applies in 2028. If not conveyed by 2028, it becomes a 2028 2nd-round pick.

2028:

  • Sixers only get DET pick if it lands between 56 and 60. Pistons must be a top 5 team for this pick to convey.
  • If Detroit is not a top-5 team, pick does not convey and expires.

2029:

  • Sixers can swap their 2029 1st-round pick with Clippers, unless Clippers pick is top-3 (protected).
