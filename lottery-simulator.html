How It Works
14 teams compete for the top 4 picks. Each team is assigned lottery combinations based on their record — worst team gets 140 of 1,000 (14%), best lottery team gets 5 (0.5%). Four balls are drawn for picks 1–4. Picks 5–14 go in reverse-record order to the remaining teams.

Odds here update automatically daily from live standings.
